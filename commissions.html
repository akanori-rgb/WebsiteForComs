Before you order

Good to know

What I draw

  • Original characters (OCs)
  • Fan art (anime, games, etc.)
  • Fandom ships & group shots
  • Monsters, creatures & fantasy beings
  • Heavy gore or graphic violence
  • Gender-neutral & non-binary characters
  • Mild romance / fluff
  • NSFW / explicit content

What I don't draw

  • Hateful or discriminatory content
  • Real people (public figures)
  • NFTs or AI training datasets

How it works

Send me a message with your idea, any reference images you have, and which tier you're interested in. I'll confirm if your request is something I can take on, give a final price quote, and we'll discuss details.

Payment is via PayPal. I require 50% upfront before starting, and the rest on completion.

Usage rights

Commissioned pieces are for personal use only. Commercial use, resale, or redistribution of the artwork (including as AI training data) is not permitted without explicit written agreement.

I retain the right to post the finished work in my portfolio and on my social media accounts.

Turnaround

  • Sketch: 3–7 days
  • Flat Color: 7–14 days
  • Full Illustration: 7-10 weeks
  • Reference Sheet: 3-4 weeks

Times may vary depending on current queue. I'll keep you updated throughout the process.

Revisions

Revisions are included as listed per tier. Major changes after the sketch phase (pose, design, composition) may require extra payment. Minor adjustments like color tweaks, expression changes, and small corrections are always included.
