About

The artist behind the pen

I'm Akanori — a digital artist with a love for good worldbuilding, stories, and animation. I draw in Clip Studio Paint, from sketchy concepts to fully rendered pieces.

My gallery is a mix of original characters, fan art, and whatever creative ideas happen to grab me. Everything tends to be "all over the place" — and I wouldn't have it any other way.
